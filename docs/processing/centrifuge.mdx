The centrifuge separates and transforms items. It can be used to extract ore dusts from ore clumps obtained from a [fragment forge](../processing/fragment_forge), spin coal dust into carbon fiber strands, or, with a [fluid addon](../processing/addons), process oil or wheat into plastic sheets.
