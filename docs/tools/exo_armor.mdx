The exo armor is a specialized suit designed to assist you on your adventures. Each piece offers unique benefits:

- **Helmet:** Provides night vision.
- **Chest Plate:** Charges your inventory items, allowing you to stay in the mines longer.
- **Leggings:** Enhances your running speed.
- **Boots:** Grants immunity to fall damage.
